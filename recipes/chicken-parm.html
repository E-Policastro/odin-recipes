Chicken Parm

This chicken parm recipe is fantastic! My mother used to make this recipe for me growing up and each bite is just full of childhood memories. Make sure to pair this with a side such as pasta or a nice italian garlic bread.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Breading
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps:

  1. Coat Chicken in eggs
  2. Dip chicken in Flour and Breading
  3. Cook That Bird all the way
  4. Enjoy the bird
