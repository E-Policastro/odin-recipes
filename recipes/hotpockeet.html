Hotpocket

Hot pockets is an easy way to get a "fresh hot" meal. But it can be tricky to cook this meal correctly if you don't know what you're doing.

Ingredients:

  • Hotpocket
  • Microwave

Steps:

  1. Put the hotpocket in microwave for recommended time on box
  2. Let it sit in microwave its too damn hot
  3. Enjoy!
